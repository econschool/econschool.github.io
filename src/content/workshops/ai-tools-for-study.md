AI assistants can be a serious aid to learning economics — or a way to avoid it.
The difference is entirely in how you use them. This workshop develops habits for
the first kind of use.

## What we cover

1. **AI as a tutor, not an oracle.** Prompting an assistant to question your
   solution, generate variations of a problem, and explain a step you are stuck on —
   without doing the problem for you.
2. **Verification habits.** Why you must check every mathematical claim an assistant
   makes, and efficient ways to do it.
3. **Workflows for study.** Building practice problem sets, summarising papers as a
   first pass before close reading, and preparing for exams.

## Who it is for

Any economics student. We will be direct about both the value and the limits of
these tools.
