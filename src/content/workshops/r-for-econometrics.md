R is the language of applied econometrics. This workshop teaches the two together:
each statistical idea is paired with the R code that implements it, so neither
remains abstract.

## What we cover

1. **R fundamentals and data frames.** Loading, cleaning, and inspecting real
   datasets with the tidyverse.
2. **Regression in practice.** OLS with `lm()`, reading the output line by line,
   robust standard errors, and what each diagnostic actually tells you.
3. **Inference you can defend.** Confidence intervals, hypothesis tests, and the
   habits that keep empirical work honest.

## Who it is for

Students with a first course in statistics or econometrics who want their applied
skills to match their theory. Prior programming experience helps but is not required.
