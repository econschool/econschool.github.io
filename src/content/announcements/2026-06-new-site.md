Econschool has a rebuilt website. Everything now lives in four places: the
[Prep Program](/prep/), [Workshops](/workshops/), [Problems & Tools](/problems/),
and [Resources](/resources/). If you are new, the [Start Here](/start-here/) page
lays out a sensible path through it all.

The site is built to last and to grow — new problems, tools, and announcements will
appear here first. If something is broken or unclear, please tell us; it helps
every student who comes after you.
